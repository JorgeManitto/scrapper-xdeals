    XB Deals - a unique Xbox Games Price Tracker

    XB Deals helps you track Xbox games prices in the official Microsoft Store simply by subscribing to any game you'd like to buy. Let's see how it works:

    1 Choose your Xbox console and region in the top menu
    2 Use search or go to the 'Explore' section to find any game you'd like to buy cheaper
    3 Press the 'Notify when price drops' button on the game page to receive free notification when the deal comes up

    That's it! Now sit back and relax - we will notify you by email when the game's price in the official Microsoft Store drops. Also check all currently available deals in 'Discounts' section and don't forget to download XB Deals iOS app
